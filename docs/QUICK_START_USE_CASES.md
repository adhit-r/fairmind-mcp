## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

1. **Be specific**: "Check for gender bias" works better than just "check for bias"
2. **Use quotes**: Put text in quotes for clarity
3. **Ask for alternatives**: "Generate counterfactuals for [phrase]"
4. **Check multiple attributes**: "Check for gender and race bias"
5. **Iterate**: Review findings and improve your content
